Layout — Option 2 (Mirrored Bottom Half)
Configuration: 13S2P = 26 cells, 2 empty slots (col 6, rows 0–1).
Top Half (Rows 0–1): S1→S2→…→S6, flowing left to right. Empty slots at column 6.
Bottom Half (Rows 2–3): S7→S8→…→S13, flowing right to left (mirrored). S7 at col 6, S13 at col 0.
Why Mirrored? S6 (col 5, top half) → S7 (col 6, bottom half) is now a 1-column hop across the gap — same distance as every other series connection. No long bus bar needed!
Polarity: Same pattern for both halves: even cols (+) up, odd cols (−) up. Series connections alternate between top face and bottom face strips.
Series Flow: S1(col0)→…→S6(col5) ↓ S7(col6)→S8(col5)→…→S13(col0). Forms a "U" shape.
Terminals: B−/PACK− (from S1, col 0 left) and PACK+ (from S13, col 0 left) — both on the left edge for clean wiring to BMS and load.
BMS Taps: 14 balance wires (B0–B13) at series junctions, extending to nearest edge.
⚠️ Wire Gauge for S6→S7: This carries full pack current (up to 40A). Use 8–10 AWG silicone wire or thick copper bus bar/strap. 22 AWG is dangerously undersized (~7A max). All series connections should be rated for full pack current.
Nickel Strips: Use 0.2mm × 30mm pure nickel or 0.15mm copper-nickel for parallel connections. For series connections (full current path), double-stack strips or use thicker stock.
Thermistor Placement (2 probes): T1 = center of top half (gap between S3/S4, rows 0–1). T2 = center of bottom half (gap between S10/S9, rows 2–3). These are the most thermally insulated spots — surrounded by cells on all 4 sides with the worst airflow. Press the NTC bead flat against the cell sidewall and secure with kapton tape. Route the thin sensor wires to the BMS NTC input.
